Energy market analysis, organized for decision-making.

Enrique works at the intersection of economic analysis, energy market fundamentals, and data products. His focus is to organize complex market information into material that can support investment, commercial, and strategic decisions.

His work covers power and gas markets, BESS, regulation, market intelligence, and tools that make energy-market data easier to read.
